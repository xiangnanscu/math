# 2.10.4 面积函数的和与差

$$
\operatorname{Arsinh}x \pm  \operatorname{Arsinh}y = \operatorname{Arsinh}\left( {x\sqrt{1 + {y}^{2}} \pm  y\sqrt{1 + {x}^{2}}}\right) , \tag{2.219}
$$

$$
\operatorname{Arcosh}x \pm  \operatorname{Arcosh}y = \operatorname{Arcosh}\left( {{xy} \pm  \sqrt{\left( {{x}^{2} - 1}\right) \left( {{y}^{2} - 1}\right) }}\right) , \tag{2.220}
$$

$$
\operatorname{Artanh}x \pm  \operatorname{Artanh}y = \operatorname{Artanh}\frac{x \pm  y}{1 \pm  {xy}}. \tag{2.221}
$$
