# 2.10.5 负角公式

$$
\operatorname{Arsinh}\left( {-x}\right)  =  - \operatorname{Arsinh}x, \tag{2.222}
$$

$$
\operatorname{Artanh}\left( {-x}\right)  =  - \operatorname{Artanh}x, \tag{2.223}
$$

$$
\operatorname{Arcoth}\left( {-x}\right)  =  - \operatorname{Arcoth}x. \tag{2.224}
$$

函数 $\operatorname{Arsinh}x,\operatorname{Artanh}x$ 和 $\operatorname{Arcoth}x$ 为奇函数,当 $x < 1$ 时, $\operatorname{Arcosh}x$ 无定义.
